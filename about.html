OUR HISTORY

GET TO KNOW US BETTER

Stepping Stones Elementary School originated from the vision of a dedicated young educator who had recently settled in Kitengela. Over the course of 15 years, under the steadfast leadership of our school director, Stepping Stones has evolved into a global educational haven. Rooted in Christian values and aligned with the CBC curriculum, our institution welcomes students from diverse backgrounds and corners of the world.
